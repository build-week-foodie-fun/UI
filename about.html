About Us

Hello everyone! We are the guys and gals who built the Foodie-Fun app. We are all Lambda-school students on a journey to become rockstar developers. Each of us at a different stage of the curriculum, were split up into groups and tasked with specific goals. We had to collaborate and work together to bring our vision to life.
